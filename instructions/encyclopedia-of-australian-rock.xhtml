The Encyclopedia of Australian Rock and Pop

An encyclopedia, in English, of persons and groups. To be converted from provided PDF files.

  1. Front matter: Only one page is provided
    • Use the text "THE ENCYCLOPEDIA OF AUSTRALIAN ROCK AND POP Second Edition - Corrected By Ian McFarlane A Third Stone Production for The Daily Flash" for a title-page
    • Use the remaining text, from "Copyright.." through the end, as copyright page.
  2. Articles: text @type is either "person" or "group".
    • Articles on Groups have a section headed "Original line-up:" after the article heading. Tag this section as a list, as shown in the example files. Tag each item as a <rilmPerson>.

      [Original line-up: Sherry Valier (real name Sherry Rich, vocals, rhythm guitar; ex-Cactus Fever), Anne McCue (lead guitar, vocals)]
      <list> <item><rilmPerson xml:id="r1"><persName><forename>Sherry</forename> <surname>Valier</surname></persName> (real name <persName type="alt"><forename>Sherry</forename> <surname>Rich</surname></persName>, <occupation>vocals</occupation>, <occupation>rhythm guitar</occupation>; ex-Cactus Fever)</rilmPerson></item> <item><rilmPerson xml:id="r2"><persName><forename>Anne</forename> <surname>McCue</surname></persName> (<occupation>lead guitar</occupation>, <occupation>vocals</occupation>)</rilmPerson></item> </list>
    • Person articles will not have the "Original line-up" section. The first sentence will repeat the name and give birth (and death) dates in parentheses.

      [Singer/guitarist/songwriter Keith Glass (17 September 1946) is one of the unsung legends...]
      <p xml:id="p1">Singer/guitarist/songwriter <rilmPerson xml:id="r1"><persName><forename>Keith</forename> <surname>Glass</surname></persName> <birth>(<date when="1946-09-17" when-custom="1946">17 September 1946</date>)</birth></rilmPerson> is one of the unsung legends...</p>
    • Both group and person articles will have a discography, headed "Albums:", and a few have a bibliography, "Further reading:". Group discographies often have sections, dedicated to invididual members of the group.

      [Albums: Going Over Old Ground (Virgin, 1989), Southerly Buster (1999); Keith Glass and the Tumblers: Living Down My Past (Virgin, 1991).]
