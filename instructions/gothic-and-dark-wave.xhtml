Das Gothic- und Dark Wave-Lexikon
  1. General comments:
    • This publication should be converted from the provided PDF
    • Articles will have a <div type="heading">, a <div type="body">, and one or two <div type="ography>.
    • Inward-facing double-angle quotation marks are used ( »text« )
  2. Front matter includes the Title page, copyright page, and Foreword.
    • Use the full title of the foreword in the <div type="heading">:
      <div type="heading"><p>VON DER UNMÖGLICHKEIT, EIN GOTHIC-LEXIKON ZU SCHREIBEN <lb />Ein Vorwort von Peter Matzke</p></div>
  3. There are four types of entry: Persons, Groups, Institutions and Terms.
    • Person entries have a comma in the heading: [BOA, PHILLIP]; [BOWIE, DAVID]
    • Group entries have not comma in the heading, but do have a "Discographie" section at the end of the entry.
    • Institution entries (mostly record companies) have no comma in the heading, and do not have a "Discography" section at the end. Most have a mailing address (called variously "Adresse" or "Anschrift" or "Kontakt") at the end and/or a "Homepage" web address.
    • Term entries have no discography or address or homepage at the end.
  4. See-references and cross references are indicated by right-pointing arrows. The title of the target article is in italic type: [1981 von → Brendan Perry und →Lisa Gerrard in Melbourne gegründet.] [DEATH CULT → Cult, The]
