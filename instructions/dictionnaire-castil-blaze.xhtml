Dictionnaire de musique modern
  1. General comments:
    • This publication should be converted from PDF.
    • All entries are <text type="topic">.
    • Entries will have <div type="heading"> and <div type="subheading"> only.
    • Double-angle quotation marks (« ») are used. See below for special treatment of block quotes.
  2. Three PDFS are supplied: "dictionnairedemu01byucast" and "dictionnairedemu02byucast" are to be used for OCR and split PDFs. However, "dictionnairedemu02byucast" is missing page 1. Please extract this page from the PDF "Castil-Blaze_tome_2".
  3. Front matter includes an imprint page (PDF p.6), title page (PDF p.7) and Préface (pp.9-20) for volume 1. Do not capture the half-title page; do not digitize front matter for volume 2.
  4. The titles of entries are given in small caps. Convert the small caps to normal case. Use for the titleStmt/title in the TEI header, and for the div type="heading".
  5. Cross references appear in some articles. They are typeset in parentheses with "V.", "Voy.", or "Voyez" in italic type:
    • ( Voyez MUANCES.)
    If there are multiple references, they are separated by commas:
    • ( Voyez CODA , TRIO. )
  6. See references are typeset the same way, but come immediately after the heading, with no other body text. Treat these according to these instructions; do not create a TEI file for the reference:
    • À BATTUTA. ( Voyez A TEMPO.)
    In the "A tempo" article:
    <textClass>
    <keywords><term>à battuta</term></keywords>
    </textClass>
  7. Block quotes are indicated in the print by a left double-angle quotation at the beginning of each line of the quotation:
    • « bats que je suis obligé de livrer sans cesse aux
      « doctrines contraires à la vérité que contient
      « cet ouvrage, où les fausses idées de Rameau,
    Tag these as <cit rend="block">, omitting the quotation marks:
    <cit rend="block"><quote>bats que je suis obligé de livrer sans cesse aux doctrines contraires à la vérité que contient cet ouvrage, où les fausses idées de Rameau,</quote></cit>]
