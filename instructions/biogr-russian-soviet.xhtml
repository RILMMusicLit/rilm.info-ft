Biographical Dictionary of Russian/Soviet Composers
  1. General comments:
    • This publication should be converted from scanned print
    • All entries are <text type="person">.
    • Omit portions of the personal name in square brackets [ ] or parentheses ( ) from the <title> element in the TEI header.
    • Entries will have <div type="body">, and one or two <div type="ography>.
    • American-style double quotation marks are used.
    • Italic type is used to indicate titles, so tag with <title> rather than <hi>.
  2. Front matter includes the Title page, copyright page, Contents, list of Abbreviations, Preface, and Acknowledgements.
    • Set the tables in Abbreviations as <table> elements each in their own <div type="section">.
  3. Personal names in the titles of entries are given in surname-forename order, in bold type, followed by birth and death date information, as available, in parentheses, and a period.
    • Tag up to the first period as a <rilmPerson> element. Birth and death information are separated by a semicolon: [Aksakov, Sergey Sergeyevich (1–6–1891, Samara; 9–4–1968).]
      <p><rilmPerson><persName><surname>Aksakov</surname>, <forename>Sergey</forename> <forename>Sergeyevich</forename></persName> (<birth><date when="1931-11-25" when-custom="1931">25 November 1931/date> in <placeName>Tampa, Florida</placeName>)</rilmPerson>. ...</p>
    • Pronunciations of name parts are given in square brackets. Tag these as <seg type="pron">.
    • Alternative names are given in parentheses. Where this occurs, enclose each name part in its own <persName> element: [Mansuryan [Muhnsuryán], Tigran Yegnayevich (Yegiayi)]
      <rilmPerson><persName><surname>Mansuryan</surname> <seg type="pron">[M<hi rend="italic">uh</hi>nsuryán]</seg>, <forename>Tigran</forename> <forename>Yegnayevich</forename></persName> (<persName><forename>Yegiayi</forename></persName>) ... </rilmPerson>
  4. Worklists are headed by the letter "W" in italic type, followed by a colon.

  6. Some articles have author names in all capitals as the last line, right justified.
  7. Cross references within articles are marked by asterisks. The text before the asterisk is the title of the target entry.
  8. See references are in the form [Name. See Name] . Only the surname of the target entry is given.
