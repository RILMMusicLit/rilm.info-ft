Biographie Universelle des Musiciens
  1. General comments:
    • This publication should be converted from scanned print/PDF
    • There are eight volumes and two supplementary volumes. The volumes have various publication dates that do not ascend in order of the volumes:
      1. 1868
      2. 1875
      3. 1874
      4. 1874
      5. 1875
      6. 1875
      7. 1867
      8. 1870
      1. Supl. 1878
      2. Supl. 1881
    • The TEI header <title> and <author> contents will be different for the two Supplement volumes. Please refer to bum-example2 file.
    • All entries are <text type="person">.
    • Entries will NOT have <div type="heading">, <div type="subheading">, OR <div type="ography>.
    • Italic type is used to indicate titles only, so tag with <title> rather than <hi>.
    • Double-angle quotation marks (« ») are used. See below for special treatment of block quotes.
    • There are brief uses of non-Latin text, including Greek and Arabic.
  2. Front matter will include a title page and Préface for volume 1, and a title page and preface for Supplement volume 1. There is no need to encode front matter files for volumes 2-8 or Supplement volume 2.
  3. Personal names in the titles of entries are given in surname-forename order. The surname is in bold type, all capitals; forename(s) is (are) in small caps in parentheses.
    • Convert the small caps to normal case.
    • Omit parentheses ( ) from the <title> element in the TEI header, and add a comma following the surname, but KEEP the parentheses in the <div type="body"> and do not add a comma.
  4. Block quotes are indicated in the print by a left double-angle quotation at the beginning of each line of the quotation:
    • [...inspirait a tous les etrangers autant d'etonne-
      ment que d'interet. « Dans mes voyages, dit
      « Burney (The present state of music) j'avis souvent
      « etonnnes des libraires du continent avec la...
      « encore de toutes les parties du monde. » ]
    Tag these as <cit rend="block">.
    <p>...inspirait a tous les etrangers autant d'etonnement que d'interet.</p>
    <cit rend="block"><quote>Dans mes voyages, dit Burney (<title>The present state of music</title>) j'avis souvent etonnnes des libraires du continent avec la... encore de toutes les parties du monde.</quote></cit>]
  5. See references are in the form [SURNAME (FORENAME). Voyez TARGET.].
    • Sometimes "Voyez" is abbreviated "Voy."
    • Where there is more than one target, they are separated by "ou" or "et" [...Voyez SAMMARTINI, ou SAN MARTINI.]
  6. Cross references within articles are set in parentheses and contain "Voy" or "Voyez" in italic type. If the parentheses contain "ce nom", the target article is the word preceding the parentheses. [... Anais (Voy. ce nom)]; target article is "Anais".
  7. Entries in the two Supplement volumes may have author initials at the end, in all caps, right aligned. Tag as <byline>.
