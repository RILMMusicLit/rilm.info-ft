Dizionario degli editori musicali italiani 1750-1930

This single-volume work in Italian contains brief articles on persons and publishing institutions in Italy.

  1. General comments:
    • This publication should be converted from scanned print
    • Articles will have <div type="heading">, <div type="body">, and <div type="ography>.
    • Guillemet- style quotation marks ( « » ) are used.
    • Italic type is used to indicate titles in body text and bibliographies, so tag with <title> rather than <hi>.
    • See-reference and cross-references are both indicated with right-pointing arrows.
  2. Front matter
    • Title page
    • Editors page: parse and tag <persName>, <forename>, <surname>
    • "Indice" (contents): tag as a two-column table.
    • "Premessa" (preface): Tag the line "b.m.a." as <docAuthor>.
    • "L’editoria musicale in Italia tra gli ultimi decenni del Settecento e i primi del Novecento":
      • Use <div type="heading"> for the title, <div type="authors"> for the name.
      • Within the <div type="body">, start a nested <div type="section"> at each of the six italic headings.
    • "Abbreviazioni e sigle": Tag as <list> elements. Begin a new one at each italic heading.
    • OMIT the quarter title page (p. 37).
  3. Entries on persons begin with a heading in bold containing a comma.
    • Use the bold text as the TEI <title> and as the content of <div type="heading">. Within the <div type="heading">, tag <rilmPerson><persName>, etc. OMIT the final period
    • Begin a new <div type="section"> in the body text whenever there is a double-line space. The first appears at the bottom of page 41.
    • Begin <div type="ography"> at the text "Fonti e bibl.:" Tag that as <head>, tag the remainder as a <list> with <item><bibl> children.
      • Items in the list are delimited in print by semicolons (;)
      • Tag italic text within each <bibl> as <title>
      • Tag unquoted roman text before the title as <author>
      • Tag quoted roman text before the title as <title type="article">
      • Tag quoted roman text after the title as <title type="periodical">
      • Most four-digit numbers less than 2001 will be <date>.
      • Except for articles in periodicals, the roman text immediate before the date will be <pubPlace> comma <publisher>.

  4. Institution entries will not have a comma in the bold heading. They are otherwise identically formatted to person entries. Make <text type="corporate">.
    • Use the bold text as the TEI <title> and as the content of <div type="heading">. Within the <div type="heading">, tag <orgName>. OMIT the final period
  5. A few entries have both a personal name and a corporate name in the heading. Treat as corporate, but do tag the name and its consituent parts. See dem-example-1.xml.
  6. See-references and cross-references are both indicated with right-pointing arrows.
    • See references are in the form [Referring Term ➙ Target Term] Treat these per http://rilm.info/ft/tei_rilm_instructions.html#see-refs.
    • Cross references in body text are indicated by a right-pointing arrow before the target term.
  7. Back matter:
    • Fonti e bibliografia
      1. tag the prose text as <div type="body"> with two sections--one from the beginning until the double-line-space on p. 373; the second until the bold head.
      2. begin a <div type="ography"> after the prose, and create a <div type="section"> beginning with each bold head.
    • Facsimile reproductions: Group the plates between pages 384 and 385 into one TEI file.
    • Case editrici musicali italiane 1750-1930: Tag as a <list>. Tag the text before the colon in each item as <placeName>.
    • Indice dei nomi: Process according to http://rilm.info/ft/tei_rilm_instructions.html#indexes.
    • Copyright page: Include the address for Società Italiana di Musicologia (two pages later) in the same TEI file.
