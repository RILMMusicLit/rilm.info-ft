Encylopédie de la musique

This title will be converted from PDF to XML plus split-PDFs. The PDFs we will supply have a text layer, but the accuracy is poor on many pages. Please create a new text layer in the split PDFs.

General notes:

  • This work consists of two parts each with multiple volumes:
    1. Histoire de la musique, 5 vols.
    2. Technique, esthétique, pédagogie, 6 vols.
    Include these titles in the <biblScope unit="part"> in the TEI header of each article.
  • The documents LAV list of articles-part I and LAV list of articles-part II enumerate the individual articles in each volume. Use the version of the title given in those documents, but use the version of the author name as it appears at the head of the article.
  • Each volume has a distinct publication year, which should be reflected in the TEI headers. *Use the publication year given on the title page of each volume, not the copyright page.*
  • Most of the titles, section heads, and personal names are in all-capital letters or small caps. Please normalize case throughout.
  • Capture footnotes in <note> elements within the body text. In print, footnote numbers restart from "1" on each page. In the TEI, please number the notes continuously through each article.
  • If there is non-Latin script – Arabic, Chinese, Greek, Hebrew, Japanese – that you cannot capture via OCR, replace it with a <gap> element with an @xml:lang attribute giving the language of the omitted text. [Le nom de Mahala מחלה, qui...]
    <p>Le nom de <hi rend="italic">Mahala</hi> <gap xml:lang="Hebrew"/>, qui...</p>
    Capture Egyptian hieroglyphs and Ethiopic characters as images AND insert a <gap> element.
  • Asterisms (⁂) are used as section breaks when there is no number or head. Omit the asterism BUT start a new <div type="section"> at its position.
  • Random pages have a copyright notice at the bottom, "Copyright by Ch. Delagrave...". Ignore these.
  • Replace quotation marks (« and ») with <q> tags; don't leave a space between the tags and quoted text.
    [...des effets « dramatiques », et...]
    ...des effets <q>dramatiques</q>, et...
  • Close up space between colons and preceding text.
