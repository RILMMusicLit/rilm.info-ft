Dictionnaire de la musique (Honegger)
  1. General comments:
    • This publication should be converted from scanned print
    • Articles will have <div type="heading">, <div type="body">, and one to three <div type="ography>.
    • French-style double-angle quotation marks ( « » ) are used.
    • In body text and worklists, italic type is used to indicate titles, so tag with <title> rather than <hi>.
    • In bibliographies, titles are in Roman type.
  2. Front matter:
    • Vol. 1: title page; copyright page; "Liste des collaborateurs" (tag as <list>); "Liste des abréviations" (tag as a <table> with two columns); "Préface"; "Bibliographie générale"; "Remarques" (set as body text, use bold in the left column as heads).
    • Vol. 2: only title page; copyright page; and "Liste des abréviations"
  3. Initial paragraphs for entries on single persons are formatted this way: [SURNAME, Forename (alt names), * place date, † place date, occupation(s) nationality.] Often, occupation and nationality are abbreviated. Dates are in DD.MM.YYYY format. Omit the asterisk and dagger as usual.
    • [ABBIATI, Franco, *Verdello (Bergame) 14.9.1898, † Bergame 22.1.1981, critique musical italien.]
      <div type="heading">
      <p><rilmPerson><persName><surname>ABBIATI</surname>, <forename>Franco</forename></persName>, <birth><placeName>Verdello (Bergame)</placeName> <date when="1898-09-14" when-custom="1898">14.9.1898</date></birth>, <death><placeName>Bergame</placeName> <date when="1981-01-22" when-custom="1981">22.1.1981</date></death>, <occupation>critique musical</occupation> <nationality>italien</nationality></rilmPerson></p></div>
    • Birth information might be indicated by "né" but is rare (this is the only instance I've seen so far): [ABELL, John, né dans l’Aberdeenshire 1653 (et non à Londres), † Cambridge apr. 1916, comp., chanteur et luthiste écossais.]
      <div type="heading">
      <p><rilmPerson><persName><surname>ABELL, <forename>John</forename></persName>, <birth>né dans <placeName>l’Aberdeenshire</placeName> <date when="1653" when-custom="1653">1653</date> (et non à Londres)</birth>, <death><placeName>Cambridge</placeName> <date when="1916-04" when-custom="1916">apr. 1916</date>, <occupation>comp.</occupation>, <occupation>chanteur</occupation> et <occupation>luthiste</occupation> <nationality>écossais</nationality>.
      Note: "all." is a nationality.
  4. Family entries will not have an asterisk or dagger or "né" in the first sentence. Individual persons' forenames are in bold type in print, normal case, and numbered. Enclose each personal name in a <rilmPerson> element.
  5. Worklists are headed "Rééditions" or "Œvres"; items separated by semicolons. Put italics in <title>, not <hi>.
    • Many worklists come in the middle of the body. In such case, end the <div type="body">, start and close the <div type="works">, then open another <div type="body">.
  6. Bibliographies are headed "Bibliographie".
    • Individual items are separated by semicolons in the print. Parts of the bibl are separated by commas.
    • Bold face type marks sections; the bold text should be used for the <head> element of each section.
    • Author names in print are typest in small caps, first initial-surname.
    • Titles are in normal case, Roman (not italic!).
    • Article titles are followed by ", in". Periodical titles are followed by vol, date.
    • Omit the dash after head text, the semicolons between items, and the final period.
    [ Bibliographie - F. G. RENDALL, F.W. G., 1858-1945, in Galpin Soc. Journal I, 1948 ; S. GODMAN, F.W. G., Music Maker, in Monthly Musical Record LXXXIV, mars-avril 1954; du meme, Fr.W. G., in Galpin Soc. Journal XII, 1959.]
    <div type="ography"><head>Bibliographie</head>
    <list>
    <item><bibl><author><forename><abbr>F.</abbr></forename> <forename><abbr>G.</abbr></forename> <surname>Rendall</surname></author>, <title>F.W. G., 1858-1945</title>, in <title type="periodical">Galpin Soc. Journal</title> <biblScope unit="vol">I</biblScope>, <date>1948</date></bibl> </item>
    <item><bibl><author><forename><abbr>S.</abbr></forename> <surname>Godman</surname></author>, <title>F.W. G., Music Maker</title>, in <title type="periodical">Monthly Musical Record</title> <biblScope unit="vol">LXXXIV</biblScope>, <date>mars-avril 1954</date></bibl> </item>
    <item><bibl><author>du meme</author>, <title>Fr.W. G</title>., in <title type="periodical">Galpin Soc. Journal</title> <biblScope unit="vol">XII</biblScope>, <date>1959</date></bibl></item>
    </list>
    </div>
  7. See references are in the form [SURNAME, voir TARGET.]"
  8. Back matter comprises tables of illustrations at the back of each volume. Set each as a <table> with three columns.
