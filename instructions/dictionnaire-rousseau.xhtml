Dictionnaire de musique par J.J. Rousseau
  1. General comments:
    • This publication should be converted from PDF. Two PDF files for each of the two volume are available; one with color page images, one black-and-white.
      • The black-and-white will be the source for the text content of the TEI files.
      • We will use the color page images for display.
    • Low and high left-facing double quotation marks ( „ ” ) are used.
    • Long s (ſ) is used throughout. Please keep these in the content.
    • All entries are <text type="topic">.
    • Entries will have no <div type="heading">, <div type="subheading">, OR <div type="ography>.
  2. Front matter will include the title page, Préface, and Avertissement for volume 1, and a title page of volume 2.
  3. Back matter in volume 2 comprises Approbation and Privilege du Roi. There are also fold-out sheets with images--in both PDFs these were scanned without unfolding and cannot be read.
  4. Titles of entries are given in the type in small capitals. Accents on capital letters are offset to the right of the character they apply to. Please convert the small caps to normal case and normalize the accents: [HYME´E; ME´DIATION; RE´SONNANCE]
    Hymeé; Médiation; Résonnance
    Tag the entry title as <term>.
  5. Words in small caps within an entry may be tagged as <term>
  6. Long quotations Long quotations include a left (opening) quotation mark at the beginning of each line of type. Omit all but the first.
  7. See references are in the form [TERM (Voyez TARGET.)] Where there is more than one target, they are separated by commas: [(Voyez SUPPOSITION, SUSPENSION.)]
  8. Cross references within articles are in the same form: (Voyez TARGET.)

Please refer also to sample files.
