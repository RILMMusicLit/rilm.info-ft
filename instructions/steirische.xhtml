Steirisches Musiklexikon
  1. General comments:
    • This publication will be converted from scanned print.
    • Double-angle quotation marks are used in the print (»word«).
    • There are entries on Persons, and entries on Geographic entities such as cities and towns. The geographical entries have no body text, but only bibliographies. (more below)
  2. Front matter includes the Title page, copyright page, Statt eines Geleitwortes, Einleitung (intro), Abgekürzt zitierte Literatur (bibliographic abbreviations) and Abkürzungen.
    • Tag the bibliographic abbreviations as a <list>; put the abbreviation in an <abbr> element. Note that names are in forename-surname order; Titles are in Roman type; publication place and date are given with no comma between.
      <list><item><bibl><abbr>MGG/2</abbr> <title>Die Musik in Geschichte und Gegenwart</title>, <edition>2., neubearbeitete Auflage</edition>, hg. von <editor>Ludwig Finscher</editor>, Sachteil, 10 Bande, <pubPlace>Kassel</pubPlace> u. a. <date>1994-1999</date>; Personenteil, 17 Bande, <pubPlace>Kassel</pubPlace> u. a. <date>1999-2007</date> (<note>ohne Nachtragsband, der noch nicht erfasst werden konnte</note>).</bibl></item>
      <item><bibl><abbr>NGROVE 2001</abbr> <title>The New Grove Dictionary of Music and Musicians</title>, <edition>2nd Edition</edition>, 29 Bande, ed. by <editor>Stanley Sadie</editor>, <placeName>London</placeName>-<placeName>New York</placeName> <date>2001</date>. - <note>Altere Auflagen werden jeweils mit dem Erscheinungsjahr vermerkt</note>.</bibl></item></list>
  3. Person entries.
    • Heading gives personal name given in surname forename order, in all bold, with no comma! Assume that the text before the first space is the surname.
    • The body text begins with birth and death date and place information, as available, marked with * and +. Tag the birth and death information in a <rilmPerson> element and link to the name via @corresp.
    • Lists of works and or writings by and about the person. Each such list with a head in all capitals should go in its own <div>.
      • Use <div type="works"> for lists headed SCHRIFTEN or WERKE;
      • Use <div type="ography"> for lists headed LITERATURE;
      • Some Bibliographies are abbreviated and keyed to the list at the beginning of the book. Tag these as <bibl> and <abbr>.
  4. Geographic Articles have headings in bold, immediately followed by a LITERATURE head. These will have only <div type="heading"> and <div type="ography">.
  5. See references are in the form [Surname Forename, s. Forename Surname].
