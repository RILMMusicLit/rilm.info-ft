Pangosmio Lexiko tes mousikes (World Dictionary of Music)
  1. General comments:
    • This publication should be converted from scanned print.
    • Double-angle quotation marks (« and ») are used throughout. All should be converted to <q> tags.
    • There are only Person and Topic articles in this publication.
    • Each article has only <div type="body">. There are no headings, and no worklists or "ographies".
  2. Front matter includes the copyright page, the title page, list of honorary board members (ΤΙΜΗΤΙΚΗ), dedication page, prologue (ΑΝΤΙ ΠΡΟΛΟΓΟΥ), and notes to readers (ΔΙΕΥΚΡΙΝΙΣΕΙΣ).
    • Set the honorary board members as a <list>. After the heading, each line in all capitals is a personal name in forename-surname order. Text in italics is the person's position and institution. Each <item> should contain <persName> and <occupation> elements.
      • ΔΗΜΗΤΡΗΣ ΑΒΡΑΜΟΠΟΥΛΟΣ
        Δήμαρχος Αθηναίων
      <item><persName><forename>ΔΗΜΗΤΡΗΣ</forename> <surname>ΑΒΡΑΜΟΠΟΥΛΟΣ</surname> <occupation>Δήμαρχος Αθηναίων</occupation></item>
  3. Person entries can be distinguished by a comma in the bold type followed by a capital letter, AND non-bold parentheses, immediately after the bold face type at the beginning, that contain at least one four-digit year. Person entries begin as follows:
    • Personal name in bold type given in surname, forename order.
    • Non-Greek names are given in Latin letters in forename-surname order. Greek names do not have this.
    • Places and dates (as available) in non-bold type in parentheses
    • The person's nationality is the first word after the closing parenthesis
    • The person's occupation is the next word.
    Enclose up to the first period in <rilmPerson> tags:
    • Αποστόλου, Γιαννης (Αθηνα, 1860/1866 - Ναπολη, 1905). 'Ελληνας τενορος, βασικό στέλεχος...
    <div type="body"><p><rilmPerson><persName><surname>Αποστόλου</surname>, <forename>Γιαννης</forename></persName> (<birth><placeName>Αθηνα</placeName>, <date when = "1860" date-custom="1860" precision="low">1860/1866</date></birth> - <death><placeName>Ναπολη</placeName>, <date when="1905" when-custom="1905">1905</date></death>). <nationality>'Ελληνας</nationality> <occupation>τενορος</occupation>, βασικό στέλεχος...</rilmPerson>...</p></div>
  4. In topic entries, there will be either no parentheses at the beginning of the non-bold type or parentheses without a date.
    • Begin with a <div type="body">—Start the first <p> of the body and tag the word(s) in bold face type as<term>.
  5. Cross references within articles are are marked by asterisks (*) following the referring term. Tag as <ref> elements and target the appropriate article file. Omit the asterisk character from the tagged text.
  6. Stand-alone see references are indicated by the word "Βλέπε" ("see") and are in this form:

    Αγγλικό κόρνο. Βλέπε Όμποε.
  7. There are two back-matter pages.
    • Set the list of portraits (ΠΙΝΑΚΑΣ ΠΟΡΤΡΑΙΤΩΝ) as a <list>.
    • Set the imprint page (ΤΟ ΒΙΒΛΙΟ) As a simple <p>.
